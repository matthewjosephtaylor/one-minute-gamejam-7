Bubble Defense

Protect the sea from the endless bubble menace!

- After setup, you will have 60 seconds to pop as many bubbles as possible

- Drag and drop sea-creature 'towers'  onto pegs

- Popping bubbles earns cash to purchase more towers

Created for the One Minute Game Jam #7
